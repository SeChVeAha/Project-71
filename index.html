School values and Achievements

Endurance: Star of the Week
endurance can help you win star of the week

The school has 6 values which are Thankfulness, Friendship, Forgiveness, Endurance, Service and Wisdom. Showing these values is a great way to increase your chances of winning the Star of the Week award.



Serivce: Punctuality
service can help your class win punctuality

The children of St Clement Danes School take great pride in showing the value Service. Punctuality is a good example of Service and can help you win the Punctuality Award.



Friendship: Leavers Awards
leavers awards are presented in year 6

The great thing about St Clement Danes School is that you can make lots of friends. The leavers awards recognise the values of Friendship.
